Rhythmic Geometry

Rhythmic Geometry is an abstract geometric motion graphic animation that combines basic shapes such as circles, squares, and triangles with contrasting colors like light blue, yellow, magenta, white, and black. The dynamic and rhythmic movements create a modern, playful, and visually balanced impression. This work represents the harmony between form and color through continuously shifting compositions, symbolizing energy, creativity, and a vibrant visual rhythm.

Asthera

Asthera was born from the idea of elegance meeting confidence. The flowing curves and bold structure of its letters tell a story of grace, strength, and self-assurance — a balance between softness and power. Set against a deep maroon background, the logo carries a sense of timeless beauty and quiet luxury. It represents more than just a name; it embodies a vision — of a brand that dares to shine with sophistication and individuality.

IKEA Summer Collection 3D Design

A bright pop of color fills the scene — playful pastels and bold primaries blend into a cheerful summer atmosphere. The display captures the essence of IKEA’s design philosophy: simple, functional, yet full of joy. Each object, from the vibrant drinkware to the quirky inflatables, invites a sense of fun and creativity. The composition feels alive, reflecting the warmth of summer and the excitement of new beginnings.

Empressa

Empressa is an homage to the rebellious spirit and fierce elegance of Cruella, capturing the raw, anarchic energy of London's punk-infused fashion scene. Rendered as a dynamic digital mixed-media piece, the artwork employs a striking palette of black, white, and dramatic splashes of blood-red, creating a visual texture that mimics the tactile intensity of acrylic brushstrokes and street spray paint. The central figure stands poised beneath the bold declaration "DEVIL," her face obscured by the defiant statement "THE FUTURE," signifying a vision that is both ruthless and visionary.

The Platinum Icon

The Platinum Icon is a powerful graphic reinterpretation of Marilyn Monroe, designed specifically for the sharp, reductive constraints of the linocut technique. This piece utilizes stark, high-contrast lines reminiscent of classic Pop Art to capture the raw drama of her iconic features and blinding star quality. The explosive halo effect behind her head symbolizes the relentless, dazzling impact of her fame and image.

Monochrome Abundance

Monochrome Abundance is an abstract study of density and light, transforming the organic form of pomegranate arils into a compelling graphic composition. Rendered in a stark black, white, and gray palette, the artwork emphasizes the jewel-like clustering of the seeds, where each sphere is a micro-universe of glossy reflection and deep shadow. The deliberate use of contrast highlights the cellular structure beneath, turning a simple fruit into a complex landscape of volume and void.

Urban Dream

Urban Dreamscape is a vibrant commentary on the dynamic intersection of daily life and imaginative escape within the modern metropolis. This mixed-media piece overlays whimsical, hand-drawn characters and playful symbols onto a photorealistic backdrop of towering skyscrapers and bustling Jakarta city streets. The floating figures—one carrying a box of aspirations, another lost in a book—represent the varied pursuits and inner worlds of individuals amidst urban grandeur. The scattered objects, from pencils to musical notes, symbolize the continuous flow of ideas, creativity, and moments that shape our experiences.

SAMYANG: Ignite

Samyang: Ignite represents a bold new chapter for the iconic instant noodle brand, reimagined for a contemporary global audience. This rebranding initiative focuses on distilling Samyang's core essence—its intense flavor and signature heat—into a sleek, modern visual identity. The new logo, prominently displayed in this mockup, features a stylized bowl of ramen subtly integrated with a vibrant chili pepper, its flame graphic embodying the brand's exhilarating spice. The clean, robust typography of "Samyang" projects confidence and quality. This design evokes a sense of both heritage and innovation, promising a refreshed experience that continues to ignite passion for flavor, connecting with a new generation while honoring the loyal fans who crave its unmistakable kick.

Nine Lives Scratchpad

Nine Lives Scratchpad is a playful, tactile Zine designed to celebrate the anarchic joy and mysterious elegance of cats through the distinctive aesthetic of Risograph printing. Using a high-contrast palette of magenta, yellow, and blue, the artwork features bold halftone textures and graphic illustrations of feline antics. The layout is intentionally dynamic, merging text sections like "Favorite Things" and "SCRATCH SQUAD" with vibrant, overlapping imagery. Crucially, the Zine is designed as an interactive piece, meant to be folded, cut, and perhaps rearranged by the reader, mirroring the unpredictable, multi-layered nature of a cat's life. It is not just a publication, but an energetic, print-textured experience dedicated to all things meow—a loud, colorful explosion of nine lives captured on paper.
