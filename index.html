Still Life 3D : "Ducklight Symphony"

This 3D still life artwork, created using Blender, features a vibrant duck-shaped balloon as the centerpiece, surrounded by supporting elements like decorative balls and elegant hanging lamps. The composition evokes a museum-like display, with focused lighting that highlights the central object and adds a dramatic atmosphere.

A uniquely shaped glass panel in the background enhances the futuristic feel, while the harmonious interplay of light and objects creates depth and balance. This piece invites viewers to appreciate the artistic potential of everyday items when presented creatively.
